## Introduction

This folder provides documentation, metadata, and data quality reports for the public Education Facilities dataset in Latin America and the Caribbean, available through the [SCL Data Lake](https://scldata.iadb.org/app/collections). It also includes exercises and scripts to facilitate analysis and exploration of these geospatial datasets. These resources are designed to help users understand the data, assess its quality, and apply it in analytical or policy-relevant contexts.

## Repository structure

- **dictionary.pdf**: provides a comprehensive data dictionary for the Educational Facilities dataset, including definitions of all variables, their data sources, accessibility status (public or private), and an overview of available variables.

- **/quality reports/**: contains quality control reports for each country, summarizing common data errors and cross-checking aggregated numbers with other sources. 

- **/exercises/**: R scripts with exercises related to the Education Facilities dataset. 

- **metadata.csv**: metadata for the Educational Facilities dataset. 

## Data source

The files in this repository are based on the Education Facilities dataset, developed by the **CIMA team (Centro de Información para la Mejora de los Aprendizajes)**, using official sources obtained either from public data or information provided directly by national counterparts. The use, reproduction, redistribution, or full or partial publication of this data layer requires **prior authorization from the CIMA team**. The dataset is available upon request via the [SCL Data Lake](https://scldata.iadb.org/app/folder/768E6E2A-DEFF-4BF9-B62D-E32518B15180). For access or further information, please contact the CIMA team: Elena Arias Ortiz (eariasortiz@iadb.org).

### About the georeferenced layer

This georeferenced layer was constructed using official datasets provided by government agencies from each country. Availability varies by country, and some sources are not publicly accessible. Below is a summary of the data sources used:

#### Argentina (ARG)
**Dirección de Información Educativa, Secretaría de Educación (2023)**  
Listado de establecimientos con características básicas (Mapa Interactivo de la Oferta Educativa).  
**Availability:** Online (not exportable)

#### Belize (BLZ)
**BEMIS, MoECST (2020)**  
Geo-schools by educational level.  
**Availability:** Not publicly available

#### Bolivia (BOL)
**Ministerio de Educación, Dirección General de Planificación (2023)**  
Instituciones educativas.  
**Availability:** Online (not exportable)

#### Brazil (BRA)
**INEP (2023)**  
Censo Escolar.  
**Availability:** Online

#### Barbados (BRB)
**Information Technology and Quality Assurance Unit (2024)**  
Government Schools Data Schema.  
**Availability:** Not publicly available

#### Chile (CHL)
**Ministerio de Educación (2023)**  
Datos Abiertos de Establecimientos Educacionales.  
**Availability:** Online

#### Colombia (COL)
**DANE (2024)**  
Educación Formal 2023: sedes educativas oficiales y no oficiales, urbanas y rurales.  
**Availability:** Online

#### Costa Rica (CRI)
**Availability:** Not publicly available (2025)

#### Ecuador (ECU)
**AMIE, Ministerio de Educación (2023)**  
Coordenadas de instituciones educativas.  
**Availability:** Not publicly available

#### Guatemala (GTM)
**Ministerio de Educación (2024)**  
Sistema de Registros Educativos.  
**Availability:** Not publicly available

#### Guyana (GUY)
**Ministry of Education (2022)**  
School data mapping.  
**Availability:** Not publicly available

#### Honduras (HND)
**Secretaría de Educación, SIPLIE (2020)**  
Georreferenciación a nivel nacional.  
**Availability:** Not publicly available

#### Jamaica (JAM)
**Ministry of Education, Skills, Youth and Information (2019)**  
Safe School Administrative Portal.  
**Availability:** Not publicly available

#### Mexico (MEX)
**Secretaría de Educación Pública, SIGED (2024)**  
Ubicación Geográfica de Escuelas del Sistema Educativo Nacional.

#### Panama (PAN)
**Ministerio de Educación (2024)**  
Directorio de Centros Educativos y georreferenciación de centros.

#### Peru (PER)
**Estadística de la Calidad Educativa (2024)**

#### Paraguay (PRY)
